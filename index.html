our mission

We Believe Every Animal
Deserves a Safe Landing.

FUZZYBUTT RESCUE was founded on a simple idea: animals get stuck, and they need help getting down — without fear, injury, or a traumatic fall. We respond to emergency calls around the clock, providing professional, humane rescues for cats, dogs, wildlife, and anything else that finds itself too high up and too scared to get down.

We're a 501(c)(3) nonprofit operated by Deadwood Climbing, a team of certified arborists who already spend their days in the canopy. Every rescue is free to the animal's owner — because when there's a pet in a tree, money shouldn't be the reason they stay there.

← 100% free to families in need.

Cat & Pet Rescues

The classic. Domestic cats are natural climbers but lousy descenders. We use specialized tree-climbing equipment to reach your kitty safely and bring them home.

Wildlife Assists

Injured birds, frightened squirrels, confused raccoons — wild animals deserve safe rescue too. We work alongside local wildlife rehabbers for proper follow-up care.

Emergency Response

Animals stuck in electrical hazards, tangled in lines, or in immediate danger. We coordinate with utility companies and respond as quickly as humanly possible.

Rooftop & Structure Rescues

Not just trees. We handle animals stuck on rooftops, window ledges, and other elevated structures. If a critter is stuck up high, we'll come figure it out.

Community Education

Prevention is powerful. We visit schools, community events, and neighborhoods to teach people how to keep their pets safe and what to do when an animal gets stuck.

Volunteer Training

Want to be the person climbing trees to save cats? We train and certify rescue volunteers with proper safety equipment, animal handling, and rescue techniques.

the process

Getting Down Is Easier
Than You Think.

01

You Call. We Answer.

Day or night, rain or shine — reach out by phone, text, or our online form. We'll get your details and dispatch the nearest available volunteer.

usually under 30 mins ↗

02

We Assess the Situation.

On arrival, we evaluate the animal's condition, the tree or structure, and the safest approach. Every rescue is different — we treat each one seriously.

safety first, always ✓

03

Up the Tree We Go.

Our certified rescuers climb using professional arborist equipment. We move slow, stay calm, and let the animal come to us whenever possible.

certified climbers 🌲

04

Reunion Time. 🎉

Safe and sound, your animal is returned to you. We do a quick health check, offer care guidance, and share in the happy moment.

our favorite part ❤️

Mango the Adventurer

Mango climbed 40 feet up an oak tree and decided that was as far as his adventure would go. Three days of coaxing, and our team got him safely down with a catch-bag and a lot of patience.

"He ate an entire bowl of food the second he was down." 😂

🦉 ⚡
WILDLIFE emergency call

The Midnight Owl

A great horned owl got a talon tangled in a communications line at midnight. We coordinated with the utility company, climbed in the dark with headlamps, and freed her within 90 minutes.

One of our proudest moments. 🖤

🐈‍⬛ 🏠
ROOFTOP 2 kittens

Pickle & Biscuit

Two 10-week-old kittens somehow made it onto a three-story roof and couldn't get back down. Both were dehydrated but otherwise fine after a very careful dual extraction.

Named them ourselves. No regrets.
